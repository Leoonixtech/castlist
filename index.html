The main cast

Eight series regulars carry XO, Kitty across its three seasons at KISS.

KC
Anna Cathcart
as Kitty Song Covey
Lead · all 3 seasons

The show's hopeful, headstrong matchmaker, chasing her late mother's footsteps to Seoul. Cathcart originated the role in the To All the Boys films and is also known for Disney's Descendants series.

@annacathcart
DK
Choi Min-yeong
as Dae Heon Kim
Lead · all 3 seasons

Kitty's long-distance first love, torn between old loyalty and new feelings. A veteran South Korean actor (Itaewon Class, Twenty-Five Twenty-One), XO, Kitty was his first English-language role.

@choi_minyeong
YH
Gia Kim
as Yuri Han
Lead · all 3 seasons

KISS's picture-perfect "it girl," secretly navigating her own identity and relationships away from her family's expectations. XO, Kitty is Kim's breakout screen role.

@thisisgiakim
MH
Sang Heon Lee
as Min Ho Moon
Lead · all 3 seasons

Wealthy, witty, and used to getting his way — until Kitty complicates his plans. XO, Kitty marked Lee's acting debut; off-screen he's an avid rock climber and Gia Kim's real-life younger brother.

@sangheonleesh
QS
Anthony Keyvan
as Quincy "Q" Shabazian
Lead · all 3 seasons

Kitty's fiercely loyal best friend and a KISS track star, working through his own KISS romance. The Filipino-Iranian American actor previously played Rahim on Love, Victor.

@anthonykeyvan
JP
Regan Aliyah
as Juliana Porter
Recurring S1 · Lead S2–S3

Yuri's on-again, off-again girlfriend, caught in one of KISS's most complicated love squares. Aliyah is also an actress, singer and dancer, and appeared in Marvel's Ironheart.

@reganaliyah
AF
Peter Thurnwald
as Alex Finnerty
Lead · all 3 seasons

KISS's easygoing young chemistry teacher, searching for the birth family he's never known. Thurnwald is an Australian actor born in Korea and adopted as a baby — a real-life echo of Alex's own storyline.

@peterthurnwald
JA
Hojo Shin
as Jiwon Ahn
Recurring S2 · Lead S3

Kitty's Korean cousin and her bridge to a family she never knew — later a KISS faculty member herself. Shin joined in Season 2 and was promoted to series regular for Season 3.

@hojoshin
